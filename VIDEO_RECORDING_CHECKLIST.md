## 🎥 RECORDING WORKFLOW

### Option 1: All-in-One Recording (Easier)

**Record everything in one take with voiceover**

1. **Start Recording**
   - Open screen recorder
   - Start recording
   - Begin with title slide

2. **Follow Script**
   - Speak clearly and at moderate pace
   - Navigate through app as you talk
   - Show each feature live
   - Pause briefly between sections

3. **End Recording**
   - Finish with call-to-action
   - Stop recording
   - Save file

**Pros**: Faster, more natural flow
**Cons**: Harder to fix mistakes

---

### Option 2: Separate Recording (Professional)

**Record video and audio separately, then combine**

#### Step 1: Record Screen (Silent)
- [ ] Record app navigation without talking
- [ ] Go through each feature slowly
- [ ] Capture all screens you need
- [ ] Record multiple takes if needed
- [ ] Save as: `smart-vendors-screen.mp4`

#### Step 2: Record Voiceover
- [ ] Open audio recording software
- [ ] Read script while watching video
- [ ] Match timing to video
- [ ] Record in sections if easier
- [ ] Save as: `smart-vendors-voice.mp3`

#### Step 3: Combine in Editor
- [ ] Import both files
- [ ] Sync audio to video
- [ ] Add slides between sections
- [ ] Add transitions and effects
- [ ] Export final video

**Pros**: Better quality, easier to fix
**Cons**: Takes more time

## 🎬 DETAILED DEMO RECORDING

**Setup:**

```
2. Ensure microphone permission granted
3. Have demo phrase ready
```

**Recording Steps:**
1. **Show Home Screen** (5s)
   - Pan across interface
   - Highlight microphone button

2. **Click Microphone** (2s)

3. **Speak Clearly** (5s)

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### Recording
1. **Do a test run** - Record 30 seconds to check quality
2. **Record in segments** - Easier to fix mistakes
3. **Over-record** - Capture extra footage, trim later
4. **Use keyboard shortcuts** - Faster navigation
5. **Smile while talking** - Comes through in voice

### Editing
1. **Cut ruthlessly** - Remove anything unnecessary
2. **Add B-roll** - Show different angles/screens
3. **Use J-cuts** - Audio starts before video
4. **Add captions** - Accessibility and engagement
5. **Export multiple versions** - Different lengths for different platforms

### Presentation
1. **Start strong** - Hook viewers in first 10 seconds
2. **Show, don't tell** - Live demo is crucial
3. **Keep it moving** - No long pauses
4. **End with CTA** - Tell viewers what to do next
5. **Be authentic** - Let your passion show

## 🚨 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### Technical
- ❌ Low resolution recording
- ❌ Poor audio quality
- ❌ Shaky cursor movements
- ❌ Too fast navigation
- ❌ Unreadable text

### Content
- ❌ Too much technical jargon
- ❌ Skipping the problem statement
- ❌ Not showing live demo
- ❌ Forgetting call-to-action
- ❌ Going over time limit

### Presentation
- ❌ Monotone voice
- ❌ Reading script robotically
- ❌ No enthusiasm
- ❌ Unclear explanations
- ❌ Missing key features
